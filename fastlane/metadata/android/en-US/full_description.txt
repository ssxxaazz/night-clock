A fullscreen, minimalist bedside clock for Android — designed for nighttime use with AMOLED-friendly burn-in protection, configurable colors, and automatic Do Not Disturb integration.

**Features:**
- Large, rotated time display (view vertically when phone is on its side)
- Sleep Mode: adjusts brightness from the ambient light sensor when available and enables Do Not Disturb (alarms only)
- Configurable colors: Green, Red, Yellow, or Blue
- AMOLED burn-in protection with an alternating checkerboard pixel mask
- Adjustable sleep mode brightness range (0-100% in 5% increments, with 100% equal to 50% of Android's maximum screen brightness)
- Always-on display
- Auto-hiding system UI

**Usage:**
- Tap screen once to reveal settings icon (top-right)
- Long-press to toggle awake/sleep mode
- Upcoming alarm time displayed at top of screen

Requires Android 15+ (API level 35).
